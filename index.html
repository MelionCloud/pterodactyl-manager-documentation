Let's Get Started!

Pterodactyl Manager

PterodactylManager® is a free, open-source extension for Pterodactyl® that allows you to manage your addons and themes from your panel.

Stop wasting your time on outdated or complicated addon installations. Just drag, drop or click!

Why PterodactylManager®

Compability Checker

No more unsupported addons that break your entire panel. With our integrated compability checker you only install the addons that are working for you.

Update Checker

We integrated an easy way to automaticly update all of your addons and themes to the latest version.

Clean addon structure

To provide the best compability and support for you addons, every addon should be build after our Developer Guidelines.

Free to use

PterodactylManager is 100% free. To support the project you can buy our addons from the market.

User Friendly

Drag and drop as well as one click installations are the future. Don't do the hard work manually.

Extendable

We provide you with the best panel extensions right out of the box. Just enable them in the addon manager.
